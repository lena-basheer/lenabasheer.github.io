01 / About

Curious about the "why" behind every number

I'm a final-year Computer Science student and aspiring Data Analyst from the Nilgiris, Tamil Nadu. Outside of dashboards, I built a full desktop application — a constraint-based timetable generator — because I wanted to see a data problem through end-to-end, from raw scheduling data to a usable export. That instinct to "ship the whole pipeline" is what I bring to every analyst role.
